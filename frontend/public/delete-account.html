NexInvo - Account & Data Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

At NexInvo (operated by Chinmay Technosoft Private Limited), we respect your right to delete your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India.

How to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@nexinvo.in
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address and organization name
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Organization/business details
  • Client information you have entered
  • Invoices, receipts, and payment records
  • All associated data stored in our systems
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please download any invoices or reports you need before requesting deletion.

Data Retention

As per Indian tax laws, we may be required to retain certain financial records (invoices, payment records) for up to 8 years for compliance purposes. This data will be anonymized and kept only for legal requirements.

Processing Time

Your deletion request will be processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.
